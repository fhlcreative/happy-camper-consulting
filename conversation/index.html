Camp Leadership Conversation

Start With a Conversation

Healthy camps are strengthened through clarity and partnership. This first conversation is simply an opportunity to listen, learn, and explore whether working together would be helpful.

Expectations

What This Is (And Isn’t)

This conversation is:

  • A time to understand your camp’s current realities
  • A chance to clarify what feels strong and what feels heavy
  • An opportunity to explore potential alignment
  • A place for honest questions

This conversation is not:

  • A sales pitch
  • A pressure moment
  • A commitment to future work

Just clarity.

Participants

Who Should Participate?

Depending on your situation, this conversation may include:

  • Camp Director
  • Board Chair
  • Executive Committee Member
  • Key Leadership Team Member

If you’re unsure who should attend, we can determine that together.

Topics

What We May Discuss

Every camp is different, but common topics include:

Mission clarity and cultural alignment

Governance and operational boundaries

Leadership expectations and accountability

Staff formation and training gaps

Safety and risk systems

Growth complexity

Recurring frustrations or friction points

We will focus where it matters most to you.

Next Steps

What Happens Next?

After the conversation, one of three things typically occurs:

1

We determine no additional action is needed.

2

We identify a focused assessment or next step.

3

We outline a structured engagement to strengthen your framework.

There is no obligation. Only clarity.

This is typically a 45–60 minute virtual conversation. You do not need to prepare anything formal. Simply come ready to share honestly. Because I work deeply with a limited number of camps each year around the globe, initial conversations help determine alignment early.

Take the Next Step

Let’s Talk

If you believe your camp can grow healthier—not just busier—I would welcome the opportunity to connect.

Before our conversation, please complete the Camp Health Snapshot via the link in the Calendly confirmation email. It takes about 5–7 minutes and helps us focus where it matters most. Your responses remain confidential and are used only to guide our conversation.
