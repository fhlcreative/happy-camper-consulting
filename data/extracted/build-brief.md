## Design Direction

### Visual Tone
- **Warm, trustworthy, professional** — not corporate, not casual
- Earthy greens and warm neutrals (aligned with camp/nature/outdoors theme)
- Photography: nature settings, camp environments, leadership moments
- Typography: Authoritative serif headings (Playfair, DM Serif) + clean sans-serif body
- Spacious but purposeful layouts — breathing room without emptiness

### Personality Keywords
- Experienced, Grounded, Trustworthy, Intentional, Collaborative
- "Not a sales pitch — just clarity" (this phrase from the site is great, keep it)

### Competitor Differentiation
- **vs The Sherry Group:** They have testimonials and a podcast but their site has placeholder text ("Describe some quality or feature...") — we can easily surpass them with polished content
- **vs Aspen Leadership Group:** More corporate/executive search focused. HCC is warmer, more relational
- **vs Generic consulting sites:** HCC has a unique proprietary framework (HCOM™ + VDMS) — lean into this as differentiator
