What This Is

Clear, Repeatable Safety Behavior

Most camps have policies. What many camps do not have is clear, repeatable safety behavior when a real moment happens.

This product helps camps build a safety culture that is trainable, practical, and visible in daily camp life.

This is not a legal manual. It is not an accreditation checklist. It is a field-ready safety package built around one core belief:

Safety Is Hospitality.
