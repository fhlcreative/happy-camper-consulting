Staff Retention Toolkit

Keep your best people coming back.

A practical retention system for camps that are tired of rebuilding culture from scratch every season.

What This Is

Intentional Systems for Staff Who Stay

Most camps assume staff turnover is just part of camp life. Some turnover is normal. But when strong staff leave year after year for preventable reasons, the camp pays for it in culture, continuity, and leadership depth.

This toolkit helps leadership teams build intentional systems for check-ins, growth, recognition, return conversations, and follow-up.
