What’s Included

Three Tools, One Package

01

Camp Health Snapshot™

A structured diagnostic tool completed online, covering six domains of camp organizational health:

  • Mission & Vision Clarity
  • Leadership & Governance Alignment
  • Culture & Value Translation
  • Systems & Accountability
  • Risk & Safety Confidence
  • Staff Development & Retention

Each domain includes scored items, reflection prompts, and a scoring guide that assigns a health tier: Healthy, Drifting, or Fragile.
